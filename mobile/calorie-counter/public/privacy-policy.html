Privacy Policy

App: HealthyMeals (Calorie Counter) | Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HealthyMeals collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account/linking data (for example, app authentication and Telegram linking data).
  • Meal data you provide: food photos, text notes, and optional voice inputs.
  • Profile data you choose to provide: age/year of birth, height, weight, activity level, goals, etc.
  • Technical data needed for app operation (for example, request logs and basic diagnostics).

2. Camera Permission

The app requests camera access only so you can take photos of meals for nutrition analysis.

  • Camera is used only after your explicit action (taking a photo).
  • The app does not use the camera in the background.
  • You can deny camera permission, but photo-based features will not work.

3. Other Permissions

  • Microphone permission is used only for optional voice input features.
  • Storage/files access may be used to save temporary app files and reports.

4. How We Use Data

  • To provide core app features (meal tracking, nutrition calculations, reports, statistics).
  • To improve result quality and app stability.
  • To provide support and prevent abuse/fraud.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers required to operate the app (for example, backend infrastructure), or if required by law.

6. Data Retention

We store data only as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data through support.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve safeguards.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will review and remove it when appropriate.

9. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting support.

10. Contact

For privacy requests and questions, contact us via Telegram bot: @CalorieJournal_bot.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
